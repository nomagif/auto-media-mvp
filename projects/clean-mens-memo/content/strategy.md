# Strategy: X complex-niche affiliate

## Positioning

A practical, non-judgmental account for men who want to improve first impressions through grooming, breath care, odor care, and simple routines.

Tone:
- calm, specific, helpful
- no shame bait
- no medical diagnosis
- no guaranteed cure
- use “may”, “can”, “often”, “basic habit” instead of definitive claims

## Why this niche first

- Broad demand and evergreen pain
- Can monetize with deodorant, oral care, skincare, supplements, grooming products, books/courses
- Less risky than explicit medical/sexual-performance claims
- Good fit for checklist/comparison LPs

## Funnel

1. X post gives one useful tip or checklist.
2. CTA sends to a neutral LP: “Clean impression checklist”.
3. LP explains routine, compares product categories, links affiliate offers.
4. Track clicks by post angle and product category.

## Offer categories to research

- tongue scraper / mouthwash / electric toothbrush
- deodorant / antiperspirant / undershirt
- face wash / moisturizer / acne care basics
- fragrance starter sets
- men’s grooming subscription / D2C brands

## Content pillars

1. Mistakes: “Things that make you seem less clean than you are”
2. Checklists: morning/night routine
3. Comparisons: deodorant vs antiperspirant, mouthwash vs tongue scraper
4. Situational: date, interview, office, gym, travel
5. Myth-busting: common overkill routines
6. Product-category education: what to look for, not a specific brand push

## 30-day target

- 5 posts/day for 30 days = 150 posts
- 1 LP live
- 3 CTA variants
- 1 daily review of impressions/clicks

## 2026-04-29 standard X image format

Use the cross-axis positioning map as the default format for product/category posts.

Default map style:
- Clear cross-axis chart, not a dense explanation diagram.
- Put 4-6 products max.
- Use intuitive axes such as:
  - 手軽 ←→ プレミアム
  - 初心者向け ←→ こだわり派
- Avoid showing "A8" or "Amazon" labels in public-facing product cards.
- Public copy should feel like genuine product guidance, not affiliate mechanics.
- Mention affiliate source only in backend notes, never as the main user-facing framing.

Default workflow:
1. Collect A8 + Amazon candidate products.
2. Pick 4-6 candidates with distinct positions.
3. Create cross-axis map.
4. Update matching LP section with natural recommendation copy.
5. Post on X with image + short intuitive explanation.
